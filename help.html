User Guide

This is for question setting app

1. Open the website and click Question Setting

2. There will be a form loaded

3. A pink marker showing when you click the map and you can input your question and answers in the form

4.Click Start Data Upload to upload data.

5.It will tell you your question has been inserted

6.Click Show Questions will show all the questions added on the map

7.You can click Remove Questions button to remove question layer

8.Daily Participation rates include all users' DPR and your own DPR. This is the DPR of all users

9.This is the User's Daily Participation Rates

10.Click Last Week Questions button will show the questions added last week

11.Remove questions added last week

12.This form shows 5 most difficult questions

User Guide

This is for Quiz App


1. Clicke Quiz and it will track will location automatically

2. Calculate the closest point and show the quiz.

3. When you choose the correct answer, it will show well done and how many question you answered correctly. Then the marker will turn green.

4. When you choose the wrong one, it will show Better luck next time.

5. Click Get 5 closest questions and the purple marker wil show 5 closest quiz points to you.

6. CLick 5 closest questions to remove question points

7. Click Ranking will you show you rank.

8. Click Top 5 Scorers will show top 5 scorers in a form.

9.Click Last 5 Answered questions will show last 5 answered questions. The questions you answered correctly are green, wrongly are orange.
